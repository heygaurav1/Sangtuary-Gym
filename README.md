# 🏋️ Sanctuary-GYM

**Your all-in-one fitness companion for workouts, nutrition, and discipline.**

---

## 📱 About

Sanctuary-GYM is an iOS fitness app designed for gym enthusiasts who want to stay consistent with their workouts, manage nutrition, and build a structured daily routine.

It helps users track their progress, plan workouts, and maintain a healthy lifestyle with ease.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🏋️ Workout tracking (sets, reps, progress)
- 📅 Daily workout routine planner
- 🍽️ Nutrition & meal guidance
- 💊 Supplement tracking
- 📊 Progress tracking & analytics
- ⏱️ Rest timer for workouts
- 🔔 Reminders & notifications
- 👤 User profile & goal tracking

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Many fitness enthusiasts struggle with:
- Lack of structured workout plans
- Poor diet tracking
- Inconsistency in routines

**Sanctuary-GYM solves this by combining all essential fitness tools in one app.**
